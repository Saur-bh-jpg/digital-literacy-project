### Project Overview
This repository contains my portfolio for the CSE0001 Digital Literacy course. In this project, I have assumed the role of a Student Digital Ambassador. The objective is to help peers navigate the digital world safely and responsibly. This repository documents my work across five core tasks: creating visual awareness resources, setting up professional online profiles, using collaboration tools, drafting professional communications, and researching cybercrime prevention.

---

### Module Summaries

**Module 1: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic**
* **Summary:** Created a one-page visual resource designed to help college students understand what digital literacy means, essential digital tools for academics, and safe internet practices.
* **Files:** Located in the `task-1-presentation/` folder.

**Module 2: Build Your Student Digital Portfolio**
* **Summary:** Established a foundational professional digital presence by setting up profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. This ensures readiness for future academic and career networking.
* **Files:** Profile screenshots are located in the `task-2-portfolio/` folder.

**Module 3: Explore Coding & Collaboration Platforms**
* **Summary:** Explored technical and collaborative platforms. Completed a beginner Python challenge on HackerRank and built a 'Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz' using Google Forms and Google Sheets.
* **Files:** Challenge and form screenshots are located in the `task-3-platforms/` folder.

**Module 4: Draft a Professional Email & Etiquette Guide**
* **Summary:** Practiced professional online communication by drafting emails for an assignment extension and a summer internship inquiry. Also developed a "Do's and Don'ts" checklist for responsible social media use.
* **Files:** Drafts and checklists are located in the `task-4-email-etiquette/` folder.

**Module 5: Cybercrime Awareness Case Study & Prevention Guide**
* **Summary:** Researched the "QR Code UPI Scam" targeting students and documented the methodology and consequences. Created an actionable prevention checklist tailored for college students in India.
* **Files:** Case study and prevention checklist are located in the `task-5-cybercrime/` folder.
